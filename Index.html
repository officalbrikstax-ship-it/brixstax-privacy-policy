Privacy Policy · Last updated June 2026
Your data stays on your device

Overview

BrikStax is a LEGO collection tracker built for personal use. We take privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Data we collect

BrikStax collects only the data you choose to enter:

  • LEGO set numbers, names, and purchase details you add manually
  • Condition, notes, and pricing information you provide
  • Collection statistics derived from your entries

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any personally identifiable information.

Where your data is stored

All collection data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. It never leaves your device except when you explicitly use the Export or Backup features, at which point you control where it goes (your email, Google Drive, etc.).

Third-party services

BrikStax fetches publicly available LEGO product data from the following services:

  • Rebrickable — set images, piece counts, and theme names. No personal data is sent. rebrickable.com/api
  • BrickSet — retail pricing data. No personal data is sent. brickset.com/api
  • eBay (via RapidAPI) — average sold prices for LEGO sets. Queries contain only set numbers and condition. No personal data is sent.

These requests are routed through a Cloudflare Worker acting as a proxy. The proxy logs are not retained beyond standard Cloudflare infrastructure logs (typically 24 hours).

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. We have no user accounts, no servers storing your collection, and no analytics tracking your behavior.

Permissions

BrikStax requests the following Android permissions:

  • Internet — required to fetch set images and pricing data
  • Camera — required for the barcode scanner feature (coming soon)

Children's privacy

BrikStax is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
